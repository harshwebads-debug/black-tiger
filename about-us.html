The Story of Strength

Who We Are

Pioneering quality and innovation in the plywood industry to build spaces that last generations.

25+

Years of
Excellence

Building Trust,
One Layer at a Time.

Founded with a vision to redefine durability in interiors, Black Tiger Plywood has grown into one of India's most trusted manufacturing brands. Our journey started with a simple belief: Quality should never be compromised.

"We don't just manufacture wood products; we engineer peace of mind for every home and commercial space."

Today, our state-of-the-art facilities utilize advanced Quad-Press technology and sustainably sourced timber to create BWP and MR grade plywood, Block Boards, and Flush Doors that stand the test of time, termites, and weather.

Our Mission

To consistently deliver premium, uncompromised, and technologically advanced wood products that empower architects, builders, and homeowners to create structural masterpieces without worrying about durability.

Our Vision

To be the undisputed leader in the Indian plywood industry, recognized globally for our commitment to sustainable manufacturing, unparalleled quality standards, and absolute customer satisfaction.

Our Core Values

Quality First

Rigorous testing at every stage of manufacturing ensures a flawless final product.

Integrity

Honesty and transparency form the foundation of our relationships with partners.

Sustainability

We are committed to eco-friendly practices and ethically sourced raw materials.

Innovation

Continuously upgrading our technology to provide the most advanced wood solutions.

Black Tiger Plywood is synonymous with strength and durability. We manufacture premium grade plywood, block boards, and flush doors engineered for generations.

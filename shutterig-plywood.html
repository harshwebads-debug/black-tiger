Shuttering Plywood

The undisputed king of heavy-duty construction. Engineered to withstand the extreme pressures of concrete pouring.

Heavy Duty Grade

Built to bear the Heaviest Loads.

Black Tiger Film Faced Shuttering Plywood is a multi-functional product designed for the construction industry. Coated with high-quality phenolic film, it ensures a smooth, mirror-like finish for concrete surfaces, reducing finishing costs drastically.

Capable of 30+ repetitions under standard working conditions.

Why Choose Our Shuttering Ply?

High Density

Weighing 34KG+ (for 12mm), it offers unparalleled structural integrity and load-bearing capacity.

100% Waterproof

Bonded with undiluted Phenol Formaldehyde (PF) synthetic resin, making it completely boiling water proof.

Maximum Reuses

High-gsm phenolic film allows for 30 to 40 repetitions, maximizing your ROI on every site.

Mirror Finish

Provides a perfectly smooth surface to the concrete, eliminating the need for extensive plastering.
